⚠️ The Problem

Citizens Face Real Barriers

Every day, millions of citizens struggle to report civic issues because of outdated, inaccessible government systems.

🗣️

Language Barriers

Government portals are only in English, leaving millions of Hindi and Marathi speakers unable to file complaints effectively.

72% of citizens can't use English-only portals
📋

Lost in Bureaucracy

Complaints disappear into the system with no tracking, no updates, and no accountability for resolution.

60% of complaints go unresolved
❓

Wrong Department

Citizens don't know which department to contact, leading to delays as complaints bounce between offices.

45% of complaints reach wrong dept.
♿

Accessibility Gap

Visually impaired and disabled citizens have no way to submit complaints through current systems.

15M disabled citizens underserved
⏳

No Transparency

Once submitted, citizens have zero visibility into complaint status, assigned department, or expected resolution time.

80% want real-time status updates
🐕

Ignored Civic Issues

Issues like stray animals, noise complaints, and sanitation problems are regularly ignored or delayed for weeks.

30 days avg. time to resolve stray animal complaint
