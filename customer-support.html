❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Find quick answers to common questions
How do I submit a complaint on CivicConnect? +
To submit a complaint, click on "Report Issue" or navigate to complaint-submission.html. Fill in the title, category, priority, description, and location. You can also attach photo evidence and use voice input for hands-free reporting. Once submitted, you'll receive a unique tracking ID via SMS and email.
Which languages are supported for submitting complaints? +
CivicConnect supports English, Hindi (हिंदी), and Marathi (मराठी). Simply select your preferred language from the pill buttons at the top of the submission form. Voice input also works in all three languages using your device's microphone.
How long does it take to resolve a complaint? +
Resolution times vary by category. Sanitation complaints are targeted for 48 hours, Water Supply and Noise within 24 hours, and Stray Animal issues within 6 hours. Roads and Infrastructure may take up to 72 hours. Urgent issues are automatically escalated if not addressed within these SLA windows.
Can I submit a complaint anonymously? +
Yes! Toggle the "Submit Anonymously" option on the submission form. Your identity will be kept confidential and will not be shared with the assigned authority. However, providing contact details allows us to send you status updates and the tracking ID.
What happens if my complaint is not resolved in time? +
If a complaint is not resolved within its SLA window, it is automatically escalated to the Head Department. You'll receive an escalation notification via SMS/email. You can also manually request escalation from the complaint tracking page. Escalated complaints are given top priority.
Is CivicConnect accessible for people with disabilities? +
Absolutely. CivicConnect is designed with accessibility in mind. It supports voice input for submitting complaints without typing, works with screen readers, has high-contrast visual design, and supports keyboard navigation throughout the platform. We are committed to inclusive governance.
